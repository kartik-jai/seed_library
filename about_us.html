Our Mission

The Seed Library is dedicated to preserving biodiversity, fostering community, and empowering individuals to grow their own food. By sharing seeds and knowledge, we help cultivate a sustainable and resilient future for everyone.

Who We Are

  • We are a passionate team from Thapar University.
  • Our goal is to make seeds accessible to all while promoting the joy and benefits of gardening. With your support, we continue to grow together.

    • What We Do

    • Provide seeds to enthusiastic gardners
    • Host educational workshops and events
    • Encourage seed saving and sharing practices
    • Promote sustainable gardening and biodiversity

    Join Us

    Whether you are a seasoned gardener or a curious beginner, the Seed Library welcomes you! Together, we can create a thriving community that values nature, learning, and connection.
